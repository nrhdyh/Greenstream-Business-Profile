Empowering Smart & Sustainable Growth

At GreenStream, we believe that innovation should be accessible to everyone — from individual researchers to students and small-scale growers. Our goal is to bridge the gap between technology and sustainability by offering affordable, cutting-edge solutions for smart farming and environmental monitoring.

What We Offer
IoT Solutions Real-time monitoring for temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and more using our plug-and-play IoT devices.
Web Applications User-friendly dashboards to track data, set alerts, and visualize trends for better decision-making.
Greenhouse Setup Affordable greenhouse planning and installation, tailored for small-scale usage and research projects.
Consultation Expert guidance for system integration, data analysis, and sustainable practices — whether you're a beginner or expert.
Target Audience Small-scale farmers, researchers, students, urban gardeners, and tech hobbyists.
Why We Stand Out
  • Affordable packages designed for small budgets
  • Customizable systems with modular IoT components
  • Free consultation for students and research projects
  • Reliable support and training to help you get started
  • Eco-friendly designs that align with sustainability goals
Our Mission

To make smart farming and environmental monitoring accessible and impactful for everyone. Whether you're conducting a school project or running a small greenhouse, GreenStream empowers you with tools that make a difference.
